Agenda

The Iberian Private Equity Conference is a conference dedicated to Private Equity Management companies, funds, venture capitals, Limited Partners, wealth managers and lawyers that facilitates a unique platform for discussions, insights, and networking opportunities. It is a one-day concentrated event with exciting keynote speakers, thrilling panel discussions, and valuable networking opportunities for CEOs, partners, managing directors among other influential business people. It is very important to the team behind conference to deliver an exclusive type of event that will provide you with an enormous value about the current trend in the financial business, in order for you to get ahead of your peers. The attendees will gain inspiration, insights, and invaluable peer-level networking opportunities. Please have a look at the 2013 edition of the Iberian Private Equity Conference agenda below.
