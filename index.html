London Design Festival at the V&A

The London Design Festival takes residence at the V&A during the Festival period. This is a unique collaboration between the world's leading museum of art and design and London's foremost contemporary design festival. In 2012 Audiences at the V&A totaled 111,538 – which is the highest ten day figure recorded in the museum’s history.

Video: The London Design Festival at the V&A

Landmark Projects 2012

Be Open Sound Portal

September 2012 saw the arrival of the BE OPEN Sound Portal in Trafalgar Square as part of the tenth anniversary of the London Design Festival 2012. Situated within one of London’s most iconic public spaces, the BE OPEN Sound Portal was an entirely new kind of installation that focused on the design that you can’t see – that of acoustics and sound – rather than visual spectacle.

Video: BE OPEN Sound Portal

Landmark Projects 2012

Mimicry Chairs by Nendo

It’s not everyday that a designer is given the opportunity to create a contemporary installation amid the splendour of the V&A Museum, which is already home to over four million items of historical and contemporary art and design. In 2012, the invitation was extended to Nendo, a Japanese studio that has emerged as one of the most dynamic design groups of the last decade.

Image: Mimicry Chairs by Nendo

    After the huge success of our last Private Equity Conferences in Switzerland and Copenhagen, a team of Master students from the University of St. Gallen, Católica Lisbon School of Business & Economics and Copenhagen Business School is now continuing this successful event this time in another country. The Iberian Private Equity Conference is going to take place on Friday 27th September 2013 in the Meliã Avenida de America Hotel in Madrid, Spain. This time many innovations will improve insights of the current market situation as well as networking opportunities.

    The Iberian Private Equity Conference is a one-day event, joining top notch keynotes and highly experienced panelists mainly from Spain and Portugal along with well-rounded global professionals from different financial backgrounds. We will have more than 300 participants from top tier Private Equity Funds, Portfolio Companies, Venture Capitalists, Limited Partners, Wealth Managers and professional advisory services such as Investment Banks, Consultancies, Law and Accounting Firms making the Iberian Private Equity Conference a unique networking opportunity in the Iberian PE space. We make it our mission to provide participants with insightful debates into key trends affecting the industries and we do this by bringing together a group of highly talented and experienced professionals.

    We welcome you to sign up for our conference and be part of this flagship event!
